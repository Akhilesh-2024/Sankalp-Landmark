About Sankalp Landmark

A legacy of transformation since 2004

Our company was started 20 years back by our founder – Mr. Uttam Appa Shinde in Nashik, Igatpuri. Under his purview, the company sold over 200 acres of land in Nashik which is a milestone in itself. As an organization, Sankalp believes not just in selling our lands and villas, but we also endeavor to welcome new members to our family and give them healthy returns.

In the future, we want to give our customers multiple options to choose from depending on their budget. We’ll be offering studio apartments and budget friendly homes in the lap of nature.

Our Projects

Discover our signature developments in Igatpuri

Mountain Bliss

Nestled along Bhavli Dam Road in Bhagat Wadi, Pimpri Sado, Mountain Bliss offers vastu‑compliant resort plots with sweeping Sahyadri mountain views. Plot sizes range from 1,000 to 5,000 sq ft, starting at ₹1,200 per sq ft.

Beautiful Bhavali

Located near Gavhande, Beautiful Bhavali features waterfront plots overlooking the dam and valley. Sizes from 1,200 to 4,000 sq ft, with prices starting at ₹1,100 per sq ft.

Explore More
Sunscape

Situated on Bhavli Dam Ring Road in Waghyachiwadi, Sunscape offers elevated plateau plots with sunrise vistas, 24/7 security, and infrastructure ready. Plot sizes 900–3,500 sq ft, starting at ₹1,150 per sq ft.
